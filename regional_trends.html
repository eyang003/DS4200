Advanced vs. Least Advanced Countries

Developing nations are disproportionately affected by climate change, with lower crop yields and higher climate risk indexes.

Regional Weather Events

Extreme weather events vary greatly by region, with certain countries experiencing severe droughts and others heavy floods.

Inputs and Yields

Regions with higher pesticide and fertilizer usage tend to have higher yields, but environmental trade-offs are significant.
